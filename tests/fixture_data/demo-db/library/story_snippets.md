# Story Snippets

## Control Plane Modernization

Slow and inconsistent developer environments were moved toward a self-service control plane with audit events, policy checks, and progressive rollout.

## Payment Reconciliation

Batch settlement reconciliation was redesigned around replayable events, idempotent consumers, and clearer operational review.

## Identity Recovery

Identity-service incident response was improved with certificate rotation tooling, admin audit logs, and recovery runbooks.

= CVAI User Guide
:toc: left
:toc-title: Contents
:toclevels: 1
:icons: font
:experimental:

CVAI is a local, single-user web app that keeps a private database of
job opportunities alongside your CV.
You paste or point it at a job posting; it extracts structured facts,
maps the role's requirements against your profile, and generates
application materials — cover letters, tailored résumés, role matrices —
all without sending your data to a third party unless you explicitly
configure an LLM provider.

TIP: For installation and initial setup, see the project
link:../README.md[README].
This guide assumes you already have the app running at
`http://localhost:8080`.

== Use cases

Ordered from simplest to most involved:

. link:guide/dashboard.adoc[The roles page] —
  overview of active roles, status pills, and open task counts.

. link:guide/ingest-url.adoc[Adding a role from a URL] —
  fetch a job posting by URL, run a quick suitability preview, then
  commit or discard.

. link:guide/ingest-text.adoc[Adding a role from pasted text] —
  paste raw job description text when the URL can't be fetched directly.

. link:guide/role-detail.adoc[Browsing a role's detail page] —
  verdict, rationale, requirement coverage table, artifacts, and event
  log.

. link:guide/tracking-status.adoc[Tracking application status] —
  record status changes with a free-form prompt; the LLM structures it
  into a typed event.

. link:guide/tasks.adoc[Managing tasks] —
  view gap tasks across all roles, close them, and reassess with the
  LLM.

. link:guide/cv-editor.adoc[Editing your CV] —
  add, edit, reorder, and remove CV entries; download the rendered PDF.

. link:guide/bundle.adoc[Generating a role bundle] —
  produce a full set of application materials: structured analysis,
  suitability report, role matrix, tailored résumé, cover letter.

. link:guide/reassess-role.adoc[Reassessing a role] —
  re-run the LLM analysis after updating your CV or closing gap tasks.

. link:guide/operations.adoc[Operations page] —
  monitor background ingestion, bundle, and reassessment operations.

. link:guide/data-privacy.adoc[Data and privacy] —
  where your data lives and what (if anything) leaves your machine.

== Regenerating screenshots

Screenshots in each guide page are generated from the demo datastore
by `scripts/docs.py`.
The devcontainer includes Playwright; run:

----
make docs
----

The script starts the server, captures each page, and saves PNGs into
`docs/images/`.

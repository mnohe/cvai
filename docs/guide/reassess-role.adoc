= Reassessing a role
:imagesdir: ../images

link:../GUIDE.adoc[← User Guide]

Reassessment re-runs the LLM against your current CV and the existing
role data, then updates the requirement coverage analysis.

Use it after you have:

* Added new experience or projects to your CV.
* Closed tasks that address coverage gaps.

== How to trigger

Click *Reassess* in the *Requirement coverage* card header on the role's
detail page.
The action runs in the background; navigate to the
link:actions.adoc[Actions page] to watch its progress.

When complete, refresh the role page to see the updated coverage table.

== What changes

The requirement coverage table, verdict, and gap tasks are updated to
reflect your current CV.

The role's structured facts — title, company, requirements — are not
re-fetched or changed.

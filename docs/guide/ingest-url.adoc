= Adding a role from a URL
:imagesdir: ../images

link:../GUIDE.adoc[← User Guide]

This is the most common workflow.
You have a job posting URL and want to pull it into your records.

Click *Ingest Role* in the top navigation bar.

image::intake.png[Intake form with URL and pasted-text sections side by side]

Fill in the *Role URL* field and click *Start URL ingestion*.

By default, *Quick analysis before full ingestion* is checked.
This runs a fast suitability check before saving anything, so you can
review the result and decide whether to proceed or discard.

image::actions.png[Actions page showing a running or completed ingestion]

While the extraction runs you are taken to the actions page, which
updates automatically — you do not need to refresh.

== Quick analysis

When the quick analysis completes, the result card shows:

* *Fit level* — High fit, Moderate fit, or Low fit.
* *Recommendation* — Apply, Apply with caveats, or Skip.
* *Matching abilities* — your strongest evidence for this role.
* *Important gaps* — unfulfilled requirements with estimated effort.

You then choose:

* *Continue full ingestion* — saves the role and generates a full
  analysis.
* *Abandon* — closes the preview; the role is not saved unless you
  continue full ingestion.

If you unchecked *Quick analysis before full ingestion*, the full
ingestion runs immediately without the preview step.
